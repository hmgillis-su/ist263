Restaurants to try in Scituate!

Scituate has many great food options, and is most known for their amazing, locally caught, seafood!

Some of the best restaurants to visit:

Salt Society

  • Food served: Sushi, seafood, burgers and other American classics
  • Although it is one of the newer restaurants in Scituate, Salt Society is already well loved
  • Located in Scituate Harbor right near the water
  • This restaurants serves incredible sushi, and fun drinks in a well decorated space
  • Takeout orders are also an option here!

A recent review:
Jenna Malley said 5/5 starts for Salt Society 3 months ago: “Really great sushi right in downtown Scituate! We sat outside and it was such a lovely day. The servers were quick and attentive and super nice. Really recommend visiting this place for a relaxing lunch or dinner! Bonus: they're dog friendly outside!”

Hibernian Tavern

  • Food served: classic American food with occasional new additions
  • Also located in Scituate harbor, Hibernian Tavern is little irish pub with a great atmosphere
  • The windows are all left open when its warm outside and there are also options to sit fully outside as well
  • There are live performances and bands some nights, and a bar
  • Reasonably priced food, but gets pretty crowded on weekends because its small inside

Lucky Finn

  • Food served: bagels, sandwiches, acai bowls and coffee
  • The most picturesque coffee shop right on the water!
  • Has a back patio over the ocean and a great view of the harbor
  • Very small inside, and the line gets pretty long on weekend mornings
  • Offers a wide selection of pastries, coffee flavors and breakfast/lunch options

A recent review:
Gabe Link said : “Such a good spot to sit and chill and/or work. Lucky Finn came as a recommendation from a friend, and it was a great place for the much needed break in my day. I had a mocha and the avocado and tomato bagel which I highly recommend. Stop by if you're in the area.”
